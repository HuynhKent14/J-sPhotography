What is J's Photography?
Metrophoto is an international wedding photography brand based in the Phillippines. It is acclaimed for its premium and highly personalized wedding photography products and experiences. Crafted with passion and precision, Metrophoto exudes a bold and glamorous spirit that is truly one-of-a-kind. Esteemed photographer Oly Ruiz leads this dynamic team of creatives to capture your every moment in perfection.
